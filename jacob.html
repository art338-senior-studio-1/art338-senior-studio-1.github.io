Course Experience

Senior Studio has helped me think more conceptually about my work and really strive to be able to explain every part of the designs I create. This is a result of heavy ideation and self-direction.

Speculative Design Project

This project is currently still in-process. I am designing for a hypothetical organization that subjects its participants to a "Dream Mass" in which they face their fears within a dream state.

Eco-Sustainability Project

With this project, I wanted to address a recycling issue that is within the University. They don't allow their takeout containers from their dining centers to be recycled. My project offers a potential solution to that problem.
