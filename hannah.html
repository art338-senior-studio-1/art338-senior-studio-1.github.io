Course Experience

Senior Studio has created an opportunity for us to ask the hard questions. Digging deeper is always encouraged to produce the best outcome for your both your project and research.

Zine Branding

This zine branding project gave me an opportunity to think about what my practice as a designer actually entails and what my interests were. I decided to create a layout that allowed for the physical opening of my work, to display my openness as a designer, and my interest in editorial and layout.

Eco & Sustainabilty Branding

By tackling the widespread issue of styrofoam, I was able to create an eco-friendly version using grass as a product. By using a completely biodegradable product, my product took a look at the second life of yard waste.

Speculative Design Project

By exploring the collision of money and happiness, I created this found object installation that allowed for viewers to go face to face with the harsh realty of what this life demands of us as humans.
