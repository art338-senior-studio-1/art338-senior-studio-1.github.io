Course Experience

Senior Studio was not what I was expecting when coming into the class, in a good way. The class pushed me harder than I would have pushed myself, allowing me to expand my design experience and ability overall.

Zine Branding

For my zine, I wanted to create something reminiscent of a magazine layout because that is the type of graphic design I am most interested in. To implement this, I included a brief description styled in the form of a letter to the editor as well as only using my work that was layout and publication inspired.

Eco-Sustainability Branding

For this prototype project I asked myself clothing, or threads of clothing. How can it be repurposed into more clothing or textile products, such as blankets and the like? Then I decided on creating yarn from these scraps that would then be repurposed back into the textiles industry.

Show Tell

Speculative Design Process
