ART 338 BFA Senior Studio

is an student centered research, design, ideation, prototyping, and manifestation space

currently investigating cultural issues
involving ecology, sustainability, and speculative design.

this site is maintained by students as a journal of their
inquiry over the course of the Fall 2021 semester at
Illinois State University, in the Department of
Graphic Design, within the Wonsook Kim School of Art.
