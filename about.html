ART 338 BFA Senior Studio

is an student centered research, design, ideation, prototyping, and manifestation space

currently investigating cultural issues
involving ecology, sustainability, and speculative design.

this site is maintained by students as a
learning device and journal of their
collective inquiry during the
Fall 2021 semester at
Illinois State University
Department of Graphic Design
within the
Wonsook Kim School of Art.
