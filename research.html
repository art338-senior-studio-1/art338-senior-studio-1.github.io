Students are currently engaging in speculative design inquiry
and prototyping while investigating the following questions:

What would the world look like without government influence?

What if humans modify their behavior rather than the planet?

What if our weaknesses are what make us powerful?

What if we were part of a monoculture?

What if art is a direct tap into our subconcious?

What if design was purely commercial and always had a meaningless meaning?

What if socially gender was removed?

How would the USA look life / function if it was powered by renewable energy?

What if design didn't encourage endless consumerism? Would it still be important?

What if all complex human problems were caused by our attempts to look towards and remedy future problems?
