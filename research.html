Students have engaged the text SPECULATIVE EVERYTHING
by Dunne and Raby. The are currently carrying out speculative
design inquiry.

Their prototypes and manifestations are investigating the following questions:

What would the world look like without government influence?

What if humans modify their behavior rather than the planet?

What if our weaknesses are what make us powerful?

What if we were part of a monoculture?

What if dreams held significantly more importance in society?

What if design was purely commercial and always had a meaningless meaning?

What is a gender neutral society?

How would life in the United States look / function if it was powered only by renewable energy?

What if there were no international borders? How would this impact travel and documentation?

What if design didn't encourage endless consumerism? Would it still be important?

What if all complex human problems were caused by our attempts to look towards and remedy future problems?

What is the future of decolonial/anti-colonial infrastructural support efforts given globalization?
